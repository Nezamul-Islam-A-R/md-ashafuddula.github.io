More about me

I myself, N. I. MD. ASHAFUDDULA earned my BSc(engr.) degree in 2018 from the department of Computer Science & Engineering at Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology (RUET), a prestigious engineering university in Bangladesh. Currently, I am pursuing my MSc (engr.) in CSE degree at Dhaka University of Engineering & Technology, Gazipur. My research interest is in Data Science, Big data, Data mining, Medical Image Analysis, and Natural Language Processing.
